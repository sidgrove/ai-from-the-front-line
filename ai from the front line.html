13 / 27

two ways software answers

Probabilistic vs. Deterministic.

Probabilistic

What GenAI
actually is.

  • Confident, regardless of correctness.
  • Outputs vary every run.
  • Optimised to convince. Not to be right.
vs.
Deterministic

What mission-critical
runs on.

  • Same input. Same output. Every time.
  • Auditable. Testable.
  • Built to be right.
